Sushi

Description

Learn how to make delicious Sushi Rolls or Maki Sushi (Hosomaki) at home with step-by-step instructions and a tutorial video. To get started, you just need a few ingredients like tuna, cucumber, nori, and Japanese short-grain rice.

These sushi rolls consist of multiple ingredients and most of them are inside-out rolls (uramaki). A lot of restaurants create their own version of combinations and unique names, but here are some common ones.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Salmon

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Roll nori
  3. Serve!
  4. Slay.
